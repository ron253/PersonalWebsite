About

I'm an entrepreneurial, creative thinker.

My Life Philosophy

I call myself an entrepreneurial, creative thinker because I love innovating. I love innovating because I believe that I can make this world a better place by putting my ideas into reality. I believe that everyone in this world has wonderful ideas that can make a positive change in this world. But what separates those that do change this world and those that don’t is whether the person believes in themselves. I believe in myself that I can and able to change this world by making small, marginal positive impacts on others. Whether that may be working to solve a humanitarian problem or volunteering in my spare time, I strive to become a better person everyday.

Why I Love To Innovate

I believe that drastic changes happen through innovation. If you take a look at all the innovations in today’s world, they were all inspired by previous inventions. An example would be Thomas Edison, for example, with the invention of the light bulb(electricity) that made modern-day inventions like computers possible, which, at the time, most people thought was “impossible”. The word “impossible” is what motivates me to innovate because I love proving others wrong that nothing on Earth is impossible. I approach everything in life as attainable and possible with a positive attitude, optimism, curiosity, and courage to explore the unknown.
