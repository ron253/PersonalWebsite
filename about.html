My Life Philosophy

I call myself an entrepreneurial, creative thinker because I enjoy innovating. Innovating to me means putting my ideas into reality. Whether that may be experimenting with new technology or refining an existing technology, I like to solve humanitarian problems through the use of technology. The most important life philosophy of mine that I hold strongly each and everyday of my life is this phrase from the Bible, "Do not work for food which perishes, but for the food which endures to eternal life (John 6:27)." This phrase to me means seeking knowlege and understanding of this world through God's given gift of being able to think, reason, and explore. Nothing is more important to me than to learn everyday and have a open-minded, world-view of this world. I do this by seeking knowledge and challenges that will allow me to develop personally, professionally, spiritually, and mentally. This is also relates to why I love to innovate because this challenges me to self-learn and adapt to changes, inspire others with my creative ideas, and stepping outside my comfort zone. I strive to do the best with what I have and approach life with optimism, positivity, and curiosity.

Why do I aspire to become a web developer?

Honestly speaking, I don't know what specific career path I want to partake, but I have an interest in doing anything that is technical and requires problem-solving. My interest right now is programming ,and web development seems to be an interest to me because it's hands-on, flexible, and allows me to do side gigs.
